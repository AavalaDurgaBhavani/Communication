1.What is Burning Desire and what is the importance of it?
	
	Burning Desire: Burning Desire Means If you want to learn anything seriously without any distractions.

	Importance of Burning Desire: Burning Desire is very important in life to learn anything perfectly. The burning desire starts when you are insulted by someone or you feel shame of you to know this simple thing. 
	If you have the Burning Desire then you learn the skill what you want seriously without any distractions. If any challenges or difficulties are occurred during the learning process then you can simply overcome that and continue your learning process.
	In that process you can't skip any day until the process would be completed. when 
you have that burning desire then you can learn anything that is difficult or moderate but you could not afraid of it. If you have that burning desire then you create the miracles with the technology.
	The difference between desire and burning desire means if desire means you stop the process when you don't have interest or any difficult situations or challenges are occurred. Burning desire means you can't stop the process until it will be completed.
	So, Everyone develop the burning desire and learn what you want and create miracles with it.
